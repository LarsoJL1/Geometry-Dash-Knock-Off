Geometry Dash Clone

This is an arcade-style game featuring two gamemodes with dynamic obstacle spawning and smooth physics.

Features:

  • Cube Mode: Jump over incoming triangular obstacles
  • Ship Mode: Rotate your ship to dodge circular fireballs
  • Portals: Random portals switch between game modes
  • Difficulty Scaling: Speed increases as you progress
  • Score Tracking: Real-time score and speed display

How to Play:

Cube Mode: Press Space or tap to jump over obstacles.
Ship Mode: Use Arrow Up/Down or click to rotate your ship and navigate around fireballs.
